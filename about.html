Bridging the gap between
academic curriculum and industry demands.

Pathway is an AI-native career intelligence engine that leverages LLMs and vector similarity search to generate hyper-personalized learning roadmaps.

The Problem

  • Generic Advice: Students rely on outdated, one-size-fits-all career roadmaps.
  • The Skill Gap: Academia moves slower than industry. Students graduate without knowing current market tech stacks.
  • ATS Rejection: 75% of student resumes are rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems before a human ever sees them.

The Pathway Solution

  • Algorithmic Matching: Using Cosine Similarity to match student skills against live job descriptions.
  • Dynamic Roadmaps: Auto-generating month-by-month execution plans using LLMs.
  • Pre-Screening Simulator: A localized ATS parser that grades resumes exactly like enterprise HR software.
